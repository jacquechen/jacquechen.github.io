  • About Me

    Architecture student by day...and by night, Jackie is an aspiring architect with a keen attention to detail. Currently based in Boston, MA, she is completing her B.S. Architecture at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. While her studies fulfill a diverse range of topics from architecture history to computation, she likes to turn her attention to topics relevant to urban/international development and landscape architecture.

    Originally from marshy South Florida, Jackie grew up in a suburban setting with access to lots of space and nature. Guided by her values in sustainability and accessibility, Jackie’s direction towards architecture manifested itself from a number of eclectic interests and experiences in the arts and sciences. Architecture is a field that has not failed to deliver in its multifaceted moments of intrigue. Furthermore, it has become an essential medium in which she hopes to re-spark in others, a child-like sense of discovery and fascination with the world we live in.
