  • About Me

    Jacqueline S. Chen (she/they) is an architecture student, ceramic artist, and aspiring maker currently based in Boston, Massachusetts. Since completing a Bachelor of Science in Architecture at MIT , Jackie is currently enrolled at the Harvard Graduate School of Design as a Masters of Architecture and Masters of Landscape Architecture candidate.                                                       

    Originally from marshy South Florida, Jackie grew up in a suburban setting with access to lots of space and nature. Her current interests stems from a desire to engage with joy and gentleness in human and nonhuman relationships. She hopes to find ways continue to spark a sense of child-like curiousity in the world.
