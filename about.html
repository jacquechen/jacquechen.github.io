  • About Me

    I am an architecture student, ceramic artist, and aspiring maker currently based in Boston, Massachusetts. Since completing a Bachelor of Science in Architecture at MIT, I have started enrollment at the Harvard Graduate School of Design as a Masters of Architecture and Masters of Landscape Architecture candidate.                                                       

    Originally from the marshy parts of South Florida, I grew up in a suburban setting with access to lots of space and nature. My current interests stem from a desire to engage with joy and gentleness in human and nonhuman relationships. I hope to find ways continue to spark a sense of child-like curiousity in the world I'm living in.
